# Guacamole

## Ingredients

* Avocado
* Lemon/Lime or its juice
* Salt
* Walnuts (optional)
* Honey (optional)

Remove pit from avocado and mash the flesh.
Add the lemon juice to soften it, and add a pinch of salt.

If you want to balance out the sourness, crush walnuts in
a mortar and add, as well as adding a teaspoon of honey. If
you want it smooth, blend the walnuts instead of grinding.
You can substitute walnuts for peanuts (more traditional) or
almonds.
